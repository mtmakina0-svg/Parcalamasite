Product Introduction

CS-40

The CS-40 is the mid-range model of our dual shaft shredder series, offering a powerful solution for industrial waste management. With its 600x600mm shredding area and dual motor system, it delivers high torque performance for shredding challenging materials. The counter-rotating twin rotors ensure controlled material feeding and homogeneous shredding results.

Why CS-40?

Its dual shaft design easily shreds hard plastics, metal scrap, electronic waste, and bulky materials. The low-speed high-torque principle ensures energy efficiency, while the automatic reverse system protects both blades and motors. It is the ideal choice for medium-scale recycling facilities.

Dual Motor System

2x11-45 kW power range delivering high torque capacity for challenging materials.

Counter-Rotating Rotors

Twin rotors rotating inward ensure reliable material pulling and shredding.

Hydraulic Coupling

Optional hydraulic coupling for overload protection and soft start capability.

Modular Blades

Bolt-on removable blade design for quick replacement and low maintenance cost.

Application Areas

The CS-40 has a wide range of applications across different industries thanks to its dual shaft design and high torque capacity. Below are the main application areas:

Recycling Plants

Pre-shredding of plastics, metals, and mixed waste. Preparing materials for granulators or sorting lines.

E-Waste Facilities

Safe shredding of computer cases, PC parts, circuit boards, and cable waste.

Wood & Pallet Facilities

Shredding damaged pallets, wooden crates, and furniture waste for biomass fuel or MDF raw material.

Industrial Manufacturing

On-site destruction of factory production waste, defective products, and packaging materials.

Technical Details

Dual Rotor System Two rotors rotating towards each other pull and shred material. Welded Hardox blades provide long-lasting performance.
Auto-Reverse System When overload is detected, the rotor reverses to prevent jamming and protect motors and blades.
Different Screen Options Interchangeable screen system according to desired output size. Options from 30mm to 100mm.
Hardox Blades Welded and bolt-on removable design. High wear-resistant Hardox 450/500 steel.

Standard Equipment

  • Body Material St-52 Steel
  • Blade Material Hardox 450/500
