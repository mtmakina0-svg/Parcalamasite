Product Introduction

TSH-100

TSH-100, endüstriyel geri dönüşüm tesislerinin vazgeçilmezidir. 1000 mm rotor genişliği ve güçlü motor optionsi ile büyük hacimli plastik kasalar, paletler, takozlar ve levhaları yüksek kapasitede parçalar. Enerji tasarrufu sağlayan hidrolik itici (Hydraulic Ram) sistemi ve dayanıklı rotor structure provides uninterrupted working performance.

Why TSH-100?

Endüstriyel geri dönüşüm tesisleri için ideal yüksek kapasite çözümü. 1000 mm rotor genişliği ile büyük hacimli malzemeleri kolayca işler. Yüksek verim, dayanıklılık ve kolay bakım özellikleri ile öne çıkar.

Compact Design

Industrial shredding solution in portable dimensions that fits small spaces.

Hydraulic Pusher

Continuous and regular shredding performance with automatic feeding.

Low Energy Consumption

30 - 75 kW motor gücü ile yüksek kapasite, ekonomik işletme.

Easy Maintenance

Quick change and minimum downtime with modular blade system.

Application Areas

TSH-100, is preferred especially in small and medium-sized enterprises due to its compact structure. Below are the main areas of use:

Plastic Production Facilities

On-site destruction of waste from injection and extrusion production lines. Plastic blocks and blocks reduced to granule size.

Printing & Packaging

Paper rolls, cardboard boxes and printing waste shredded and made ready for recycling.

Textile Workshops

Converting fabric pieces, yarn waste and textile scraps into fiber form.

Small Recycling Facilities

Ideal starter machine for recycling facilities operating in limited space.

Technical Details

Hydraulic Ram Provides continuous and regular feeding by pushing material to the rotor. No operator intervention required, works automatically.
Smart PLC & Auto-Reverse The rotor automatically reverses under overload, protects blades and prevents material jamming.
Replaceable Screen Screen system that can be easily changed according to desired granule size. 20mm - 80mm range options.
Hardox Blades 60-80 high wear-resistant Hardox 500 steel cutting tips. Long-lasting and replaceable.

Standard Equipment

  • Body Material St-52 Steel
  • Blade Material Hardox 500
  • Drive System Geared Motor
