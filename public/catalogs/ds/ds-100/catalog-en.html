Product Introduction

DS-100

The DS-100 is an industrial-scale four shaft shredder. With its 1000x1000mm wide shredding area and 4x22-45kW motor power, it offers high-capacity waste processing. It delivers excellent results in shredding plastic, rubber, tires, and composite materials.

Why DS-100?

Four independent drive units guarantee uninterrupted operation with all materials. Automatic reverse system prevents blockages. Preferred for e-waste, tire, and heavy plastic applications.
